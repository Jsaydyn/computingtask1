2 cups flour
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 eggs
3/4 cup milk (180ml)
2/3 cup vegetable oil (160ml)
1 cup strawberries, hulled and chopped - From the Garden if in season
3 tablespoons fresh mint, chopped - From the Garden if in season

Method:

1. Preheat oven to 200C
2. Grease and flour medium muffin cups or line with paper patty tin cups.
3. Add sifted flour, sugar, baking powder and salt to a large bowl and make a well in the centre.
4. Make an egg mixture by combining egg, oil and milk in a bowl or jug. Mix until combined.
5. Add the egg mixture to the well in the dry ingredients and stir until mixed. Do not over-mix or dough will be heavy. Stir in strawberries and mint.
6. Pour into muffin cups and bake for 20 to 25 minutes until done. (Hint- Do not turn your oven up to speed up the cooking process - this will make your muffins brown on the outside and uncooked in the middle!)
